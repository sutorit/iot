# Privacy Policy for Sutorit

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Sutorit. Sutorit represents a revolutionary IoT ecosystem. We prioritize user privacy and efficiency. This Privacy Policy explains how our application functions without holding your data captive on third-party servers.

## 2. Zero Account Registration
Our application necessitates zero registration and zero sign-up. The system is completely open yet inherently secure, protected solely by local, device-level passwords, thereby safeguarding data from vulnerable cloud databases.

## 3. Data Ownership and Cloud Access
Unlike conventional smart devices that mandate cloud accounts, Sutorit bypasses corporate cloud limitations. 
* Sutorit allows the user to configure their own complimentary MQTT/Firebase credentials. 
* The application primarily operates using Firebase. 
* Upon pairing the application, the URL and secret key are automatically retrieved from the device and stored in the application's memory. 
* This grants enterprise-grade remote access without incurring any recurring monthly fees.

## 4. Local App Permissions and Device Integration
To facilitate seamless device integration, the app requires local permissions to communicate with your Sutorit hardware:
* **Network Scanning (Radar):** By opening the application and selecting the '+ (Add New)' function, the application's integrated radar interface automatically scans the vicinity for nearby Sutorit devices. 
* **Local Storage:** Selecting the detected device and entering its unique password securely stores all credentials on the user's mobile device. 

## 5. Offline Operation
Operation of Sutorit requires neither a residential router, an active internet connection, nor a cloud account. 
* Upon initial power-up, the device establishes its own secure, standalone Hotspot. 
* Connecting to this network with a smartphone grants immediate access to the full interface for appliance control. 
* The hardware autonomously executes scheduling commands directly.
